Steps in Preparation of making Brownies:
1.Start preparing chocolate,melt the chocolates.
2.Now melt the butter in the same bowl.
3.Stir the both ingredients properly.
4.Add sugar to the cooled chcolate-butter mixture.
5.Stir it with wooden spoon until the sugar is completely incorporated.
6.Add 3 eggs to the mixture .
7.Add required amount of vanilla extract.
8.In a separate bowl,start mixing the flour and baking soda.
9.Add the flour mixture to the chocolate mixture all at once.
10.Start mixing the batter.
11.Add chopped nuts,mint chips or coco powder and make it stir properly.
12.Transfer the batter made to the container and keep it in the oven.
